设a,b,c∈R+,abc=1a,b,c\in R_+,abc=1a,b,c∈R+​,abc=1证明：

∑1a3(b+c)≥32\sum\frac1{a^3(b+c)}\geq\frac32 ∑a3(b+c)1​≥23​

有：

∑[1a3(b+c)+a(b+c)4]≥∑1a\sum[\frac1{a^3(b+c)}+\frac{a(b+c)}4]\geq\sum\frac1a ∑[a3(b+c)1​+4a(b+c)​]≥∑a1​

故：

∑1a3(b+c)≥∑1a−∑ab2\sum\frac1{a^3(b+c)}\geq\sum\frac1a-\sum\frac{ab}2 ∑a3(b+c)1​≥∑a1​−∑2ab​

=∑1a−∑12c=∑12a≥12×3abc3=32=\sum\frac1a-\sum\frac1{2c}=\sum\frac1{2a}\geq\frac12\times\frac3{\sqrt[3]{abc}}=\frac32 =∑a1​−∑2c1​=∑2a1​≥21​×3abc​3​=23​
